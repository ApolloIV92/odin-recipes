Chicken Fajitas

Description

These are simple chicken fajitas. I recommend using a cast iron skillet if you have one, but a regular frying pan will work as well!

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast (1 lb for 2 people)
  • Bell peppers
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • Tortillas
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cumin
  • Cheese
  • Sour cream

Directions

  1. Chop vegetables.
  2. Slice chicken into thin strips.
  3. Heat up oil in pan or cast iron skillet.
  4. Cook vegetables in pan.
  5. Set vegetables aside, cover to keep warm.
  6. Season and cook chicken.
  7. As chicken finishes cooking, warm tortillas.
  8. Place vegetables and chicken in tortillas.
  9. Top with cheese and sour cream.
