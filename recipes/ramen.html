Ramen

Description

This is a version of instant ramen with additions from your own pantry to make it more interesting. Most of the Ingredients are optional, depending on what you have on hand!

Ingredients

  • Ramen packet
  • Leftover meats- chicken, beef, etc
  • Vegetables- onion, mushroom, green onion, etc.
  • Sesame Oil
  • Soy Sauce
  • Hardboiled egg

Directions

  1. Start water to boil for ramen and another pot for egg if hard-boiling.
  2. Chop vegetables, and chop any meat being used into small pieces.
  3. Sautee vegetables before adding to ramen.
  4. Put egg to boil for 6.5 minutes.
  5. Put in ramen noodles, seasoning packet, soy sauce/sesame oil.
  6. If hard-boiling egg, when done, place into bowl of ice water for 3 minutes.
  7. When noodles are soft, remove from heat.
  8. If using hard-boiled egg, slice in half and add to ramen.
