Salami Sandwich

Description

This is a very basic salami sandwich. I have eaten this for lunch almost every day for years. You can make this as simple or as complicated as you like.

Ingredients (optional in bold)

  • Salami
  • Bread
  • Cheese
  • Mayonnaise or other sauce

Directions

  1. Toast bread, if desired.
  2. Add mayo or other sauce if desired.
  3. Put salami on bread.
  4. Close sandwich and eat.
